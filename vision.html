our vision
<<<<<<<<<<<<>>>>>>>>>>>>
Ovrplyd is a Bangalore based independent music record label with the objective of forming a collective of various music artists collaborating and combining talents. Ovrplyd was created outside the mainstream box of indie record labels, and aims to give underrated music artists a platform for their voices to be heard and the chance to be a part of something larger than themselves.

The involved artists will have to pay the company on an annual basis, but will not have to provide a cut from their streaming royalties.

Ovrplyd is not just another label, it’s a community that elevates and guides young individuals who are talented and passionate about music but don’t have the right access to exposure and opportunities.

To dominate the independent music scene. We strive to discover and bring together all independent artists and bring them all under one powerful collective so that we can show everyone just how much noise we all can make. A indie music label made by independent artists.

Artists at OVRPLYD strive to change indie norms and pioneer the new mainstream where everyone gets their moment in the spotlight. No one gets left behind.

At OVRPLYD the Artist is what makes US and not the other way around.

We strive to create a community which isnt restrictive and promotes experimentation and collaboration. The possibilities are boundless and we dont need to limit our potential.
